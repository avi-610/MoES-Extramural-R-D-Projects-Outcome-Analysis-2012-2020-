# MoES-Extramural-R&D-Projects-Outcome-Analysis-2012-2020-
Outcome analysis of 234 EMR projects across 5 thematic areas under the Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), Government of India - covering research outputs, collaborations, and societal impact through data analysis and Power BI dashboards.

**Overview**
Government-supported outcome analysis study conducted by The Energy and Resources Institute (TERI) for the Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), Government of India.
The study evaluated 234 completed Extramural R&D (EMR) projects spanning 2012 to 2020 across five thematic areas — Ocean Sciences, Atmospheric Sciences, Geosciences, Hydrology & Cryosphere, and Seismology
to assess research outcomes, institutional contributions, and societal implications.

**Business Problem**
With 400+ EMR projects supported over the years, there was limited structured visibility into the collective research outcomes, institutional performance, and societal impact across thematic areas. 
This study was designed to provide a data-driven assessment framework to measure project effectiveness and research ecosystem contribution under MoES.

**My Contribution**
* Performed data cleaning and transformation to ensure accuracy and consistency of project-level data.
* Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) across thematic areas, institutions, and research outputs.
* Developed interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize project outcomes, trends, and performance metrics.
* Assisted in report writing and structuring analytical findings for the official government report.

**Methodology**
Data Collection → Data Cleaning & Transformation → Exploratory Data Analysis → Power BI Dashboard Development → Insight Generation → Report Writing

**Key Parameters Analyzed**
* Thematic area-wise project distribution
* Year-wise project completion trends (2012–2020)
* Institutional & university contributions
* Research outputs — publications, patents & citations
* Research collaborations & partnerships
* Societal implications & knowledge dissemination

**Skills**
Data Cleaning & Transformation, Exploratory Data Analysis, Power BI, Data Visualization, Survey Analytics, Performance Benchmarking, Report Writing, Data Storytelling

**Impact**
Contributed to a comprehensive government outcome assessment providing structured, data-driven insights into 234 MoES-supported EMR projects,
enabling evidence-based policy decisions and future R&D planning under the Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India.
